Admission Eligibility:
The admission to P.E.S.S program in the Department of Physical Education and Sports Science is centrally administered through an “Admission Committee” of Daffodil International University. Applications are invited twice in each year from qualified candidates who passed the HSC or equivalent examinations with a minimum of 2.50 out of GPA 5.00. The applicants need to pass a qualifying examination (Out of 100 marks) with a minimum core (total 50 marks) in all the sections. The candidates who obtain a very good mark in other subjects except for English need to go through a comprehensive English course under the Department of English of Daffodil International University. Beside these strategies we continually upgrade this entry level exam to ensure meritorious students get the opportunity to be nourished by their merit in the Department of PSSS.
Total minimum credit requirement to complete the program:
The Total Number of Courses for awarding a B. Sc. degree in Physical Education and Sports Science (PESS) are 58 and the total credits are 144.
Total Class Weeks in a Year:
28 Weeks in a Year / 14 Weeks in a Semester (Excluding other activities and assessment)
Minimum CGPA requirements for graduation: CGPA 2.0
Maximum Academic Year of Completion: Within three academic years.
